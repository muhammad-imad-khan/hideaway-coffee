Our Story

A genuine
escape in the
heart of Soho.

Hideaway Coffee House sits in Smith's Court — a sunlit courtyard tucked behind Brewer Street that most of the city has never noticed. We like it that way. Since opening our doors, we've been a home-away-from-home for Soho locals, creatives, and anyone who needs a proper breather.

We take coffee seriously. Our beans come from Origin Coffee Roasters, one of the UK's most respected specialty roasters, and every cup is made with care by a team who genuinely loves what they do. Whether you're in for a quick flat white or a long afternoon with a book, we're glad you found us.
